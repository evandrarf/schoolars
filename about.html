About Us

Schoolarsh is a scholarship website that focuses solely on helping students find scholarships can be an excellent resource for those seeking financial assistance for their education. It can save students time and effort by streamlining the scholarship search process, and increase their chances of success by providing valuable resources and support.

Vision

Schoolarsh should have a comprehensive database of scholarships available across various fields of study, academic levels, and geographical locations. The database should be searchable and filterable by various criteria.

Mission

Schoolarsh can help alleviate the financial burden of higher education for students from all backgrounds, by providing a reliable and efficient resource for accessing scholarship opportunities.

Since 2023 we help students all over the world

We help students find and apply for scholarships that can help cover the cost of their education. With provide a centralized database of scholarships available to students from a variety of sources.
